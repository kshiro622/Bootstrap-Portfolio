About Me

Hi, I'm Katherine, but most people call me Katie.

I'm looking to move into the web development world. After working as an inner-city high-school math teacher for 3 years, I developed a passion for education. I know the potential technology has, that it provides teachers and classrooms with the toolsets that are essential to meeting the needs of every student, and I want to lend my skills to the growing edtech scene here in Chicago.

In my free time, I'm an avid restaurant goer, I play ultimate frisbee on a competitve women's club team here in Chicago (called Dish), I'm big into watching Chicago sports (Go Cubs!) and I love to read (my latest novel was Alexander Hamilton).
